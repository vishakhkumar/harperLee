Why we used Twitter to measure Harper Lee's impact on modern America

After Harper Lee's death in February 2016, people from different age groups and different locations around the world mourned the loss of a great writer. As we looked through tweets with the tags #HarperLee, #RIP and #TKAM, we saw how "To Kill A Mockingbird" continues to influence America's view on race half a century later.
